Preparation

Planning = key. I started to play around with the idea of how the app should work with a pencil and paper, on the sofa, while watching a tv show. My initial idea looked like this:

The app should focus on female customers buying clothes for themselves or mothers buying also for their children. But what if somebody just finds out about the products and wants to buy only presents? Well I ended up with these three female "personas" and decided to just divide the app in three categories represented by tabs - 'for children', 'women dress' and 'accessories', adding a 'latest stuff' tab as an overview and the checkout.

An App

Design is inspired by original Google's MD with focus on the AMANITA logo pattern and base color to express the simple red/white/black feel the logo has. After experimenting with Loto as base font a bit, I kept Roboto in various weights and sizes in place.

​(Impression renders done with Blender's Cycles, original designs made with Figma.)
