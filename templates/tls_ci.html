Corporate Identity and brand guidelines

When I started doing basic designs for TLS - Boca Systems a logo was already in use across all products and offices, but there were no guidelines set and the logo application overall was still random. Due to fast growth of the company there was a need for rules. After some time I've started with setting simple guidelines for logo placement, fonts used and layout options.




As these kept evolving over the years, I got the assignment to summarize everything and write the company's corporate identity and brand strategy document. This was a huge task but after several meeting sessions the final document eventually saw the light of day.
