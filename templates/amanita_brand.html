Not a company. "Just" a real brand.

AMANITA exists since 2014, led by young, crafty A. Kosca. She makes beautiful handmade cloths and accessories. With an inspiring choice of materials her products stand out in the crowd and are perceived as 'real'. No serial machinery is involved in production, she does everything by hand in her small workshop on the 3rd floor of a regular apartment building. Supported by her boyfriend, daughter and pets Huxley and Toby.
